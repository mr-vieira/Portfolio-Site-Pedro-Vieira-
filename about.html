About Me

I'm a UX Designer and optimistic problem-solver, with a passion for sustainable design.

I Believe in using design as a force for positive change. By understanding human behaviour and the complex systems that make up the world, we can use it to generate solutions to the most defying challenges.

Starting as a Graphic designer, I've worked with different talented teams, helping them uncover insights and translate them into strong visual concepts.

As someone who cares for the lifecycle of his shared creations, I've started to develop an ever growing interest for sustainability, and got the chance to assist teams through eco-design challenges.

From all these collected experiences, one thing I've learned is that collaboration is the ultimate design tool. There's no better way of solving a problem than having a diverse team with multiple perspectives.

Now, combining my graphic design background with human-centered design practices, user-research, and creative problem-solving, I'm on a quest to design efficient, equitable, and sustainable digital products.
