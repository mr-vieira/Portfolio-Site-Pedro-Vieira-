Viv Platform

A health and wellbeing web app designed to be a go-to tool when it comes to manage people‚Äôs daily health issues.

Project
Overview

Problem

Whether caused by the always-on lifestyle, information overload, stress at work or life in general, people are struggling to maintain a balanced and healthy lifestyle.

Companies and HR departments often provide their staff with access to web platforms containing general physical and mental well-being features. Yet, these portals tend to underdeliver by not considering user diversity and a varied set of user needs.

Solution

An inclusive web portal designed to help health-conscious individuals to be more in control of their health and wellness, by allowing them to record and manage health and medical information, access general physical and mental wellbeing features and to better comunicate with medical professionals.

The Challenge

What's standing in the way?

The main goal for this project was to provide health-conscious users with a solution that could help them overcome barriers to a healthier lifestyle. Pretty ambitious, right?!

But the angle here was to focus on removing what's standing in the way of a healthier life. Rather than trying to add new content and features for the sake of doing so, contributing even more to information overload.

High Level Goals:

  1. Help users to feel in control of their health.
  2. Design an inclusive App, reaching the highest number of users who can benefit from it.
  3. Be there for users, making the solution available wherever, whenever.
  4. Build a sense of trust and reliability.

User Research

Interviews

After gaining a better perspective of the problem space, it was time to engage with potential users and identify their actual needs and goals. To that effect, a user research was conducted by means of interviews.

Research Goals

  1. To understand users on a deeper level regarding their health: what their habits are, what routines, goals, obstacles.
  2. Get to know users‚Äô opinions on existing Apps and competing platforms.
  3. Collect data that may validate previous assumptions.
